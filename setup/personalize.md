# Personalize Your Garage
### The First Thing You Do After Setup

---

Your garage is ready. Now you need to set it up for your business.

Claude reads your `CLAUDE.md` file at the start of every session.
The more it knows about you, the more useful it is. Right now it has
placeholder text like `[YOUR NAME]` and `[YOUR PM SOFTWARE]` — that needs
to be your real information.

This takes about 10 minutes.

---

## How to Do It

Open Claude Code. Copy the prompt below. Paste it. Hit enter.

Claude will ask you questions one at a time. Just answer in plain English.
When it's done, your `CLAUDE.md` will have your real information and every
session from here on will feel like Claude already knows your business.

---

## The Personalization Prompt

> I just set up my Wolf Pack garage and I'm ready to personalize it.
>
> Please read my CLAUDE.md file first.
>
> Then walk me through filling in all the blank fields — ask me one
> question at a time. Don't show me a big form. Just ask, wait for
> my answer, then ask the next one.
>
> The fields I know you'll ask about:
> - My name and company name
> - Roughly how many units I manage and what city/area I operate in
> - My tech stack: PM software, email, team chat, phone system, texting tool,
>   lease signing tool, payment system, accounting software, maintenance tool,
>   process/workflow software
>
> Do not ask me to list my individual properties. My portfolio changes constantly
> and my PM software is the source of truth. When you need property or tenant data
> for a specific task, ask me to pull a report or export from my PM software at
> that time.
>
> After you have all my answers, update the CLAUDE.md file with my real
> information. When you're done, show me a summary of what changed.
>
> Then ask me: "Your garage is ready. What do you want to build first?"

---

## What Happens Next

Once your CLAUDE.md is filled in, Claude will:

- Know your name and company automatically
- Know which tools you already use
- Suggest integrations based on what you actually have
- Stop giving you generic advice that doesn't fit your business

**That's the whole point.** This isn't a demo. This is your real setup.

---

## If You Want to Update It Later

Just tell Claude:

> "My tech stack has changed. I switched from [old tool] to [new tool].
> Please update my CLAUDE.md."

Claude will find the right section and update it.
